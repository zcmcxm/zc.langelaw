Nostalgia seems to be something unavoidable.

I'm from Chongqing, a municipality located in southwestern part of China. It's famous for being the origin of hotpot (火锅) and a "fog city", so I wrote 勒是雾都, which means this is the fog city in Chongqing dialect.

I miss my hometown, my families and friends there.

I'm grateful for everything happened there in my first 16 years.

Beautiful days.
