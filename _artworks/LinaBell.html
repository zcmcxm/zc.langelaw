LinaBell is a new character in Duffy & Friends toyline at Disney Parks. She is a fox with an inquisitive mind and the intelligence that supports it. She finds joy and excitement in solving problems and mysteries.

I really like this adorable fox! She brings me a lot of positive energy and happiness. She is so endearing that I wrote this for her.

“放轻松，就当漫游地球”

To match with the cuteness of LinaBell, the style of the words is very cute which is quite different from that of my previous works.
